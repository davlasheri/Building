Product Range

Everything
in One Package

A Velocity Build kit is a single coordinated procurement. Every component is specified, sourced, and matched to your structure — eliminating interface errors and procurement delays between trades.

Wall Panels

High-density PIR/PUR and Rockwool insulation cores. 40 mm to 200 mm thickness. Precision-formed steel facings with concealed or visible fastener profiles.

  • PIR / PUR / Rockwool cores
  • U-values to 0.10 W/m²K
  • Full RAL colour range
  • Fire Class B-s1, d0 rated

Roof Panels

Corrugated and standing-seam structural profiles with integrated thermal breaks. Engineered for snow, wind, and dynamic loads. Leak-proof panel-to-panel joints.

  • Standing seam profiles
  • Integrated thermal break
  • Snow load to 3.0 kN/m²
  • Min 3° pitch compatible

Structural Steel & Frameworks

Hot-rolled primary columns and rafters, cold-formed secondary purlins and girts. Pre-punched, galvanised, delivered with full connection hardware.

  • S355 hot-rolled sections
  • Pre-drilled & part-matched
  • Z/C purlins included
  • EC3 / AISC compliant

Completing Elements

Colour-matched ridge caps, corner trims, parapet flashings, window/door surrounds, and structural hex-head fasteners. Nothing missing on delivery day.

  • RAL-matched flashings
  • Structural hex fasteners
  • Ridge & valley caps
  • Sealants & closures
Technical Advantages

Why Velocity Build?

Three non-negotiable pillars that every project is measured against.

Global Material Sourcing

We source exclusively from certified EU and international steel mills and insulation manufacturers. Every batch carries mill test certificates, CE marking, and third-party fire-test documentation — no compromise on provenance.

  • CE & ETA certified products
  • Full mill test certificates
  • Traceability on every panel

Exceptional Thermal & Fire Resistance

Rockwool-core panels achieve REI 60/120 fire resistance ratings. PIR cores reach U-values down to 0.10 W/m²K — cutting operational heating and cooling costs by up to 40% versus traditional masonry envelope systems.

  • REI 60 / REI 120 fire rated
  • U-value to 0.10 W/m²K
  • Up to 40% energy cost reduction

Drastic Labour Savings

Pre-engineered lock-together panel systems require no specialist masonry trades. A 1,000 m² structure can be fully enclosed in under 5 working days with a small erection crew — dramatically reducing program duration and on-site labour costs.

  • No masonry trades required
  • 1,000 m² enclosed in <5 days
  • Full erection manuals provided
